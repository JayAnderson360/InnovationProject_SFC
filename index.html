Who We Are

Custodians of Sarawak's Natural Heritage

Sarawak Forestry Corporation is dedicated to the conservation and sustainable management of Sarawak's rich biodiversity and forest ecosystems. Established to protect the state's natural treasures, we are committed to balancing environmental preservation with responsible eco-tourism and community development.

Our Values

To conserve and manage Sarawak's forest resources and biodiversity through sustainable practices that benefit both nature and communities.

Our Vision

To be an agency of excellence in the conservation of Sarawak's wildlife and its totally protected areas for all people, for all time

Our Mission

To create, maintain totally protected areas and to conserve wildlife through innovation and best practices for the equitable benefits for all
