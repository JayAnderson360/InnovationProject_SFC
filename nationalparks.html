Our National Parks

Sarawak is home to numerous national parks, each offering unique experiences and showcasing the state's rich biodiversity. From ancient rainforests to stunning limestone formations, these protected areas are vital for conservation and eco-tourism.

Bako National Park

Sarawak's oldest national park, known for its diverse wildlife and stunning coastal landscapes.
