What We Do

Sarawak Forestry Corporation is committed to preserving the incredible biodiversity of Sarawak through sustainable forest management practices and proactive conservation efforts. Our comprehensive approach includes:

Forest Conservation

Managing protected forests and implementing sustainable practices to preserve Sarawak's rich biodiversity.

Wildlife Protection

Safeguarding endangered species through habitat conservation, anti-poaching initiatives, and rehabilitation programs.

Sustainable Development

Balancing economic growth with environmental preservation to ensure the long-term health of our ecosystems.

Community Engagement

Working with local communities to promote sustainable practices and preserve traditional ecological knowledge.

Logo Rationale

The Sarawak Forestry Corporation logo embodies the essence of our state's natural heritage and our commitment to conservation. Each carefully designed element represents a vital aspect of our mission and the biodiversity we protect.

The Leaf

The leaf symbolizes the foundation of Sarawak's rich flora diversity. It represents our ongoing efforts and core responsibility in preserving the state's plant biodiversity and the ecosystems they create.

The Hornbill

As Sarawak's official State bird, the hornbill gives our logo a distinctive Sarawakian identity. This magnificent and rare species represents our pride in the state's natural heritage and our commitment to avian conservation.

The Orang-utan

The orang-utan represents the incredible diversity of wildlife in Sarawak. It symbolizes our dedicated conservation efforts to protect all wildlife species, with special focus on endangered animals that face threats to their survival.

The Tree

The tree stands as a powerful symbol of hope and sustainability, representing a bright and thriving future for all generations. It embodies our vision that through conservation, we secure the natural world for those who will come after us.

Governance & Integrity

Our commitment to transparency, accountability, and ethical practices

At Sarawak Forestry Corporation, we uphold the highest standards of governance and integrity in all our operations. We believe that strong governance is essential for effective conservation and sustainable management of Sarawak's natural resources.

Sarawak State Integrity Circular

We adhere strictly to the Sarawak State Integrity Circular, which provides comprehensive guidelines for ethical conduct and integrity in all state-affiliated organizations.

The circular outlines principles and practices that ensure transparency, accountability, and integrity in our operations, decision-making processes, and interactions with stakeholders.
