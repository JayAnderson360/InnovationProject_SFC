Wildlife Conservation

Orangutan Adoption Programme

Sponsor an orangutan and contribute directly to their care, rehabilitation, and eventual release back into the wild. Your adoption helps fund feeding, medical care, and habitat protection at Semenggoh Wildlife Centre.

Bring Back the Rainforest Programme

Participate in hands-on reforestation efforts to restore Borneo's precious rainforest ecosystems. Work alongside our conservation team planting native tree species in degraded forest areas.

Explore Sarawak's only freshwater swamp forest and its unique seasonal lake ecosystem. Enjoy boat tours, birdwatching, and traditional fishing demonstrations.

Explore Now
Island Adventure

Lakei Island

Discover this protected island's beautiful beaches, rainforest trails, and diverse marine life near Santubong. Includes snorkeling, nature walks, and beach clean-up activities.

Bird Watching

Piasau Nature Reserve Hornbill Watch

Observe Sarawak's state bird, the magnificent hornbill, in its natural habitat at this urban oasis in Miri. Guided tours offer excellent photography opportunities.

Join Watch

Sama Jaya Nature Reserve Night Walk

Experience the rainforest at night in this urban green space in Kuching. Discover nocturnal creatures with our expert guides and learn about their unique adaptations.

Explore this unique ecosystem in Kubah National Park dominated by rattan species. Learn about their ecological importance and traditional uses by local communities.
