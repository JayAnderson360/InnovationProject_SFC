About This Tool

This comment rating system uses NLTK's VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary for Sentiment Reasoning) to analyze the sentiment of text comments. Based on natural language processing, it assigns a rating from 1 to 5 stars:

  • ⭐ - Very negative sentiment
  • ⭐⭐ - Negative sentiment
  • ⭐⭐⭐ - Neutral sentiment
  • ⭐⭐⭐⭐ - Positive sentiment
  • ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ - Very positive sentiment
